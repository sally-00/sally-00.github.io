## Outreach and public engagement

I actively communicate robotics research to audiences across academia, industry, and the public through talks, live robot demonstrations, workshops, and community activities. Alongside research, I value building spaces where robotics is more accessible, collaborative, and engaging across disciplines and career stages.

### Public engagement and demonstrations

In recent years, I have delivered research presentations and live robot demonstrations to diverse audiences at [Cambridge Festival](https://www.festival.cam.ac.uk/) 2025, ICRA 2024, [IFE (International Food and Drink) Manufacturing](https://www.ife.co.uk/) 2024, and [REAP (Realising the Economic and Agricultural Potential conference)](https://www.agri-tech-e.co.uk/reap-conference/) in 2023. These activities focused on communicating both technical research outcomes and their potential real-world applications across academia, industry, and the public.

I have also taken part in school-facing outreach activities, including robotics engagement at Hardwick and Cambourne Community Primary School in 2023.

### Community and professional activities

- Leader, [Women in Robotics Cambridge](https://sites.google.com/view/wir-cambridge/home), from February 2026 to present: building partnerships with Robotics Inclusive and UK-RAS STEPS to create networking opportunities across robotics communities and roles, and planning future networking activities and workshops.
- Member, [Women in Robotics Cambridge](https://sites.google.com/view/wir-cambridge/home), from July 2024 to present: maintaining the website and LinkedIn presence, and supporting the organization of networking activities and workshops including the Robotics Networking Event supported by the UK-RAS network and Robotics Inclusive, Cambridge Festival at Newnham College, and the CHIA Robotics and Embodied Workshop.
- Member, [AgriFoRwArdS CDT Advisory Board](https://agriforwards-cdt.blogs.lincoln.ac.uk/cdt-role/advisory-board/), from June 2023 to present: contributing feedback on events, student communications, and alumni engagement.
- Member, [AgriFoRwArdS CDT Student Panel](https://agriforwards-cdt.blogs.lincoln.ac.uk/cdt-role/student-panel/), from March 2023 to present: representing student perspectives on CDT events and initiatives, including summer schools, annual conferences, and seminar series.
- Co-Chair, Autonomous Systems breakout session, Embodied Intelligence Conference 2025.

- “Virtual model control for manipulation and path planning under uncertainties” — oral presentation at UKACC PhD Showcase 2024, London.
- “Controller-based Reinforcement Learning in Robotics Manipulation” — poster at TAROS 2023 / Joint Robotics CDT Annual Conference 2023.
